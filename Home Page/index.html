05
Secretary General's Address

Kushagra Yadav

We are gratified to invite you to the 5th Edition of the pioneering Delhi Public School, Eldeco Model United Nations, taking place on the 9th and 10th of August 2020.
Being organised at the premises of our esteemed institution, Delhi Public School, Eldeco, Lucknow, the Secretariat calls on you to be a part of this peerless occasion.
With almost 50 minds involved over the past 5 years, the innovative ideas helped DPSEMUN evolve into one of the most reputed conclaves of the circuit. With 6 modern-day agendas adapted by a blend of conventional and unorthodox committees, DPSEMUN proffers delegates a plethora of opportunities and challenges unlike any other. Supervised by the most experienced and decorated Executive Board members we promise to broaden one's horizon of knowledge. With traces of having the largest press corps, each and every moment of yours will be recorded for perpetuity.
We aim at heading towards an ecological and environment friendly event and to support the underprivileged in order to actually make a change of a mindset. With collaborations to various NPOs and NGOs we take our small steps towards sustainable development and are expecting you to be a join us.
So, this August use your diplomacy as your weapon and your research as your ammunition and the be the recipient of one the most aesthetic conferences. This August turn your argument into an opinion and leave the hospitality to us.

This August witness the change. This August be the change.

तत्परिवर्तनं भव

Kushagra Yadav
